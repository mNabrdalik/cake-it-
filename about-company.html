About company

brief history

The company was founded in 1976 by two bakers: Peter Barrett and Robert Scott. From the very beginning, their main idea was to make old proven recipes, passed on from generation to generation and make cakes only from natural ingredients.

The company currently has 3 confectioneries in Seattle where you can order your dream cake or eat and drink something on site.
